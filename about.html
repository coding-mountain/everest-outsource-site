About Us

Everest Outsource Inc. is a software development and outsourcing company serving clients across Israel, South Africa, Nepal, the Netherlands, Canada, the United States, and India.

We work with organizations that need more than extra hands. Our role is to provide capable engineering support, clear communication, and delivery discipline across the product lifecycle. From new product builds to modernization efforts and long-term team augmentation, we focus on work that improves execution quality and reduces delivery friction.

Our services cover software engineering, user experience design, testing, DevOps, security support, data systems, and AI integration. We tailor engagement models to the stage and needs of each client, whether that means a focused project team, ongoing delivery support, or dedicated technical capacity embedded alongside internal teams.

Where We Work

We support client relationships across North America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, with active coverage in Canada, the United States, the Netherlands, Israel, Nepal, India, and South Africa.
